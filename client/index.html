ListenSync

What's this?

This is the first web application that lets you synchronize audio playback between devices. Each device you add will act as a new speaker for the music you love!

Howto

1 First, enter a room.

2 Once you're in a room paste any YouTube video URL into the input at the bottom of the screen, or... Paste it just anywhere to add it to the playlist (yeah, just do Cmd+v or Ctrl+v!).

3 Open the chat and have fun with your friends!
